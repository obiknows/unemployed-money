### 🤔 trade idea: swap 🌴 AMZN 50DTE CCS (Call Credit Spread) 🐻💳 for a similar play but in 🖥 XLK (technology sector ETF)

#### reasoning (just like seasoning, but for an idea to become real. necessary)

rather than short individual equities which come with their own independent risks such as news, etc, its prolly easier to take the trade from the single stock level to a whole basket of stocks (which also contains the single stock).

when i did the risk analysis, there was more premium in the credit so my trade looks more like a 4:1 (credit received : collateral risked) which is way better than the 1:1 I was taking with the single equity play.

Using that same amount of buying power in a play like this, gives me $1600 max profit as opposed to $400.

with the same buying power.

#### plan

close the 🌴AMZN play when market opens, open 2-4 contracts of the 🖥 XLK short using the **same** amount of buying power.
