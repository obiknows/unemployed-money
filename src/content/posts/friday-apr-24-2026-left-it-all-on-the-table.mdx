### WHAT MY SYSTEM ACTUALLY IS

while i'm at it — let me write this down
so it exists somewhere real.

**the 30 minute ORB (opening range breakout)**
draw the high and low of the first 30 minutes
of the RTH session. that's the battlefield.

**ICT Power of 3**
accumulation, manipulation, distribution.
the first break of the ORB is usually a fake.
a run on stops. liquidity grab.
*wait for the manipulation before entering.*

**FVGs (Fair Value Gaps)**
identify the first presented FVG of the session.
look for retracements and inversions back into it.
always watching SPY for macro confirmation.

**the entry**
after manipulation — the fake break —
price reverses. that's the entry.
not before. not during. after.

simple. clean. mine.
