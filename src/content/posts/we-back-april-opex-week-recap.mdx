## POST GAME 💰⛹🏿‍♂️

### SO WHAT DID WE LEARN

* **Risk management is the only thing you can control.**
  the market does what it does. you can't control direction.
  you CAN control how much you lose when you're wrong.
  i let that slip Monday and paid for it all week.
* **Fat fingers happen. How you respond is the trade.**
  the fat finger wasn't the problem. holding it and then
  revenge trading was the problem.
* **Friday showed what's possible when you're nimble.**
  3 trades, 3 wins, opex Friday. that's the blueprint.
  in and out. no ego. no holding.
* **TSLA is not my friend right now.**
  took too many entries on TSLA this week going against
  the move. gotta let that one breathe and wait for
  better setups.
