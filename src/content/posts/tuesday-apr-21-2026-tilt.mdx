## POST GAME 💰⛹🏿‍♂️

##### SO WHAT DID WE LEARN

* **THE FIRST LOSS IS ALWAYS THE CHEAPEST.** -$493 on
  META at open should've been the end of the day.
  everything after that was recovery trading. that's
  not a strategy.
* **FLIPPING BIAS WITHOUT A NEW THESIS IS GAMBLING.**
  going from META puts to META calls in the same hour
  with no new information is not trading. that's
  emotion with an options account.
* **14 TRADES IS NOT DISCIPLINE.** my green days average
  3 trades. today i took 14. the number alone tells
  you what was happening mentally.
* **THERE NEEDS TO BE A HARD STOP RULE.** if i'm down
  more than X by noon, the laptop closes. non-negotiable.
  building that rule right now.
