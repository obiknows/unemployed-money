## POST GAME 💰⛹🏿‍♂️

##### lets glean some insights

- **Set It and Forget It (WALK AWAY)** — it makes more sense now to set the trade up, set the SL (stop loss), TP (take profit), and walk away. Tightening stops changes the POP (probability of profit) by reducing the area that price can be in without stopping out the trade. It's even worse when you move stops into profit ***before*** the trade hits TP. I know moving stops to breakeven still makes sense, but trailing the stops today ***cost me money***, even though the whole ideology behind moving the stops is in fact to SAVE MONEY.

- **1-3 TRADES MAX (DON'T OVERTRADE)** — I have a tattoo that reminds of this rule but the gist is that you can't really make more than 1-2 large emotional decisions in a day, so in order to trade more *efficiently* and prevent overtrading, it makes sense to have an absolute limit on trades. 1-3 max per account / day. Or maybe just in general. Idk. but for now, just 1-3 on the TopStep per day. can't be out here overtrading.
