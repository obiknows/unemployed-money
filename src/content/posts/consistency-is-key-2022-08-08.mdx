but i know myself.

so i know that having *defined risk* built-in helps me with trade psychology by taking that decision off the table.

plus the fact that managing options, specifically (credits spreads) to me are easier when comes to looking at P/L (profit-loss), being affected by market volatility, news, and thats not even all of it.

depending on how you structure your positions, you can get paid for the time in the trade on top of being correct on the markets direction.

so theres a mathematical edge to using options that you can gain and I'd be crazy to learn all of this and not try it out.

so i opened some positions.
